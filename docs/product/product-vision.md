# Product Vision

## Purpose

JP2 App is a digital ecosystem that helps the Order communicate publicly, guide interested candidates, support brothers in daily life, and give officers a simple operational panel.

It must serve the full V1 funnel:

```text
Guest / Wife / Interested Person
  -> Interested Candidate
  -> Authenticated Candidate
  -> Brother
  -> Officer
```

## North Star Questions

The product should help a brother quickly answer:

| Question | V1 feature |
| --- | --- |
| Who am I in the Order? | Brother Profile |
| What is my chorągiew? | My Chorągiew |
| What is important today? | Brother Today |
| How should I pray? | Prayer Library |
| What is the next event? | Events |
| What is my next formation step? | Formation Roadmap |
| Are brothers praying together right now? | Silent Online Prayer |

## Product Principles

- The app opens without login and gives public value.
- Candidate experience is guided but does not expose brother-only content.
- Brother experience is calm, practical, and privacy-preserving.
- Admin Lite supports V1 operations without becoming an ERP.
- Visibility and permissions must be testable from the first backend phase.
- The system must not rank spiritual life or expose private participation.

## V1 Outcome

The V1 pilot is successful when one chorągiew can:

- publish public prayers and public/family events;
- receive join interest requests;
- invite and guide candidates;
- give brothers a useful daily companion;
- publish audience-scoped announcements and events;
- review roadmap submissions;
- host public and brother silent prayer sessions;
- manage the above without direct database edits.

## Content Caveat

All official descriptions of the Order, candidate requirements, formation language, and prayers require Order approval or pastoral/content approval before production release.
